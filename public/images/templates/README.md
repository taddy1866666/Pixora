# Photo Strip Templates

## 📁 Upload Your Templates Here

Place your photobooth strip template images in this folder.

### Naming Convention:
- `strip-1.png` - Single photo template
- `strip-3.png` - 3 photo vertical strip
- `strip-4.png` - 4 photo vertical strip
- `horizontal-4.png` - 4 photo horizontal layout
- `square-4.png` - 4 photo square/grid layout
- `strip-6.png` - 6 photo vertical strip

### Template Requirements:
- PNG format with transparent areas for photos
- Recommended resolution: 300 DPI for print quality
- Photos will be placed in designated areas
- Templates can include borders, frames, branding, etc.

### How It Works:
The system will:
1. Load the template image as background
2. Overlay captured photos in designated positions
3. Apply filters and effects
4. Generate final photobooth strip

## 🎨 Template Design Tips:
- Leave clear rectangular areas for photos
- Add decorative borders/frames
- Include branding space
- Use high contrast for visibility
- Consider print margins
